Detailed Description

template<std::size_t Dim>
struct vulpes::fe::internal::WarpAndBlendNodesPolicy< Dim >

Compute the warp&blend nodes on a simplex in Dim dimensions See: Warburton, Tim. "An explicit construction of interpolation nodes on the simplex." Journal of engineering mathematics 56 (2006): 247-262.

The nodes enjoy better interpolation properties than the equi-spaced ones, in particular a much smaller Lebesque constant.
