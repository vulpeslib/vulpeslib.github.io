(a Versatile and parallel compUtational Library for high-order discontinuous Polytopal Element Simulations) is an open-source, general-purpose library for the numerical solution of multiphysics and multiscale differential problems with Discontinuous Element methods on polytopal meshes.

The research group



Vulpes is developed at MOX - Laboratory for Modeling and Scientific Computing, Department of Mathematics, Politecnico di Milano, as part of the research activities conducted within the ERC Synergy Grant NEMESIS.

The Principal Investigator of the Politecnico di Milano node of the NEMESIS project is Paola F. Antonietti
