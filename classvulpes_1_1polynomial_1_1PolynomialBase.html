Detailed Description

template<typename T, std::size_t Dim, typename Polynomial>
class vulpes::polynomial::PolynomialBase< T, Dim, Polynomial >

Base template class for a general polynomial.

There are two main kind of polynomials depending of their storage: dense and sparse. Dense ones are available in 1D, 2D, and 3D. Sparse one are available in any dimension.
