Detailed Description

template<std::size_t Dim, typename DofPolicy, typename NodePolicy>
class vulpes::fe::FeSpaceLagrangeP< Dim, DofPolicy, NodePolicy >

General finite element space on a simplex with (nodal) Lagrangian basis function, can be either continous or discontinuos.

The basis function can be either the classic Lagrangian interpolan on equi-spaced nodes or the spectral warp-and-blend basis by Warburton and Hestaven that is proven to have a much better conditioning.

The continuos/discontinous variant are enforced by the DofPolicy
