Vulpes

Vulpes (Discontinuous and VirtUaL Polytopal ElementS method) is an open-source, general-purpose library for the numerical solution of multiphysics and multiscale differential problems with Discontinuous and Virtual Element methods on polytopal meshes.

The research group

Vulpes is developed at MOX - Laboratory for Modeling and Scientific Computing, Department of Mathematics, Politecnico di Milano, as part of the research activities conducted within the ERC Synergy Grant NEMESIS.
