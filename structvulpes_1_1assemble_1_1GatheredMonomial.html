Detailed Description

template<std::size_t Dim>
struct vulpes::assemble::GatheredMonomial< Dim >

Represents a monomial after the "gathering" process for face integrals.

Template Parameters
DimThe spatial dimension of the problem.

A monomial from a face expression involving a neighbor cell is "lifted" into a higher dimensional space (that is the space where the extra variables are the parameters of the physical (cartesian) mapping of the cell). This struct helps to factorize and group terms of this lifted monomial.

E.g., in 2D we have P(x, y, j1, j2, t1, t2) where X = (x, y) are physical coordinate, J is the diagonal of the Jacobian and T the translation. Then, P must be integrated in the X variable and evaluated in the J and T variables. We write P as \( P = X^\Alpha * J^\Beta * T^\Gamma * (\sum c_k * J^\Delta_Jk * T^\Delta_Tk) \) where \(\Alpha, \Beta, \Gamma, \Delta_Jk, \Delta_Tk\) are multi-indices
