Detailed Description

template<std::size_t Dim, typename DofPolicy>
class vulpes::fe::FeSpaceModalQ< Dim, DofPolicy >

Modal FE Space on polytopal elements (tensor product of Legendre polynomials on the bounding box). The total degree is limited to the given order in the sum of the exponents.

Parameters
