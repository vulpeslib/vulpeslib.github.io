From the global descriptors of a dof, get the dof in local cell enumeration.

Parameters
meshthe mesh
cell_idglobal id of the cell in cosideration (with MPI is the (global) id in local proc enumeration)
owning_enty_idid of the geometric entity that owns the dof (e.g. the dof may belong to a vertex, an edge, a face or be internal to the cell)
enty_dimdimension of the entity, that is this specify if the entity considered in the previous parameter is a vertex (0), and edge (2) etc.
enty_multiplicityid within the entity, e.g. to an edge might belong more than one dof, this indicates which is the id within these
